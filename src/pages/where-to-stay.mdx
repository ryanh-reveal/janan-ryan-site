## Pick your base by style

Choose the area that matches how you want your trip to feel.

> ### Central convenience
>
> Best for guests who want easy access to restaurants, transport, and event meet-up points.
>
> - Easy walking access to key city spots
> - More hotel options
> - Can be noisier on busy nights

> ### Quiet neighborhood feel
>
> Better if you prefer calm evenings and local character over central buzz.
>
> - Usually more spacious stays
> - Better for longer stays
> - May require rideshare or bus for late returns

## Booking strategy

> ### What to prioritize
>
> - Distance to main wedding events
> - Cancellation flexibility
> - Transport options after evening events

> ### What to avoid
>
> - Booking without checking hill/walk accessibility
> - Assuming late-night public transport availability
> - Choosing price alone without location context
