## Entering New Zealand

Let's make sure you can legally enter

> ### Visitor's Visa
>
> Along with a valid passport, you're also going to need a visa to enter New Zealand
>
> - Visit [immigration.gov.nz](https://www.immigration.govt.nz/visas/new-zealand-electronic-travel-authority-nzeta/)
> - More than likely you'll be eligible for an NZeTA - it allows a 3 month stay, processes within a few days, and is reasonably priced

> ### Flights and layovers
>
> You'll likely be flying into Auckland and then swapping to a domestic flight down to Wellington
>
> - The flight from Auckland to Wellington is about an hour
> - Make sure you have enough time to connect - the internation terminal is a separate building from the domestic terminal
> - Here's a handy [youtube video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ql0uhaVI_W4) showing how to get navigate the airport
