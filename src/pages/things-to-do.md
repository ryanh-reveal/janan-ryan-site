## If you have half a day

Pick one nearby activity and keep the pace relaxed.

> ### City classic route
>
> - Waterfront walk
> - Museum stop
> - Coffee and late lunch in Te Aro

> ### Scenic route
>
> - Viewpoint or coastal walk
> - Snack stop nearby
> - Early return for evening plans

## If you have a full day

> ### Day trips near Wellington
>
> Great for guests extending their stay by one or two days.
>
> - Wine region or countryside outing
> - Coastal town visit
> - Nature reserve experience

> ### Weather-proof alternatives
>
> - Museum and gallery plan
> - Long lunch and cafe hopping
> - Indoor markets and shopping lanes

## If you are adding extra NZ travel

> ### Short extension (2-4 days)
>
> - Pick one destination and avoid over-transit.
> - Prioritize one key landscape experience.
> - Build buffer before international departure.

> ### Longer extension (5+ days)
>
> - Combine one city and one nature region.
> - Use domestic flights strategically for time savings.
> - Confirm weather and driving conditions by season.
